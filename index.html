What We Treat

Comprehensive Neuropsychiatric Services

We offer evidence-based treatment across a wide spectrum of neurological and psychiatric conditions with personalized care plans.

🧠

Neuropsychiatric Evaluation

Comprehensive assessment of cognitive function, behavior, and neurological health using advanced diagnostic protocols.

😟

Depression & Anxiety

Evidence-based treatment for mood disorders, panic attacks, and anxiety using modern pharmacological and therapeutic approaches.

💤

Sleep Disorders

Diagnosis and treatment of insomnia, sleep apnea, narcolepsy, and other sleep-related neurological conditions.

⚡

Epilepsy Management

Advanced seizure management with personalized medication protocols and lifestyle guidance for optimal quality of life.

🎯

ADHD & Cognitive Issues

Specialized assessment and treatment for attention disorders, memory problems, and executive function impairments.

🤝

Psychotherapy & Counseling

Individual and family therapy sessions using CBT, mindfulness, and trauma-informed therapeutic approaches.

Our Specializations

Advanced Psychiatric Skills & Expertise

We bring specialized knowledge and cutting-edge treatment approaches across a diverse range of psychiatric disciplines.

🚨

Acute Care & Emergency Psychiatry

Immediate intervention for psychiatric emergencies, crisis management, and acute mental health episodes with round-the-clock availability and rapid stabilization protocols.

🤝

Consultation Liaison Psychiatry

Specialized psychiatric support for patients with medical and surgical conditions, addressing psychological factors affecting medical outcomes and patient recovery.

⚡

Electroconvulsive Therapy (ECT)

Safe and effective treatment for severe depression, making catatonia, and treatment-resistant psychiatric conditions using modern anesthesia and monitoring techniques.

🏥

Psychiatric Rehabilitation Services

Comprehensive rehabilitation programs promoting recovery, functional restoration, and social reintegration for individuals with chronic mental health conditions.

🌍

Community Psychiatry

Community-based mental health services, public awareness initiatives, and preventive psychiatry focused on promoting mental wellness at the population level.
